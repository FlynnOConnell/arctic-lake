# Agarose Protocol

1) measure *1.23 grams of agarose*, low melting point specificially

100 mL distilled water 

- Sonicate the bead sample
- Pre-made, stored in the dark, good for a few months

2) add agarose to water
- parafilm, put a small hole in the top to microwave
- microwave for 1min, don't melt agarose, probably actually shouldnt use parafilm at all @raghav?
- can boil, check every 15s
- swirl around, check for undisolved particles

5:1 agarose/bead
