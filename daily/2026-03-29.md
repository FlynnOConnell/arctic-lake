## Notes

● clusterCD (volumetric dF/F):
  - Baseline F0 = rolling 30th percentile across a temporal window (±10 timepoints via localCR)
  - Formula: ((F - F0) / (F0 + offset) + 3) * 5000 → stored as uint16
  - Applied per-voxel independently across the whole 3D volume
  - Offset = 10% of mean baseline (stabilizes division near zero)
  - Output is a full 3D volume at every timepoint

  Suite2p dF/F:
  - First extracts per-ROI traces: F[roi, t] = mean intensity of pixels in each detected ROI
  - Neuropil subtraction: Fc = F - 0.7 * Fneu (removes contamination from surrounding tissue)
  - Baseline F0 = rolling percentile (default 8th percentile in suite2p, 20th in LBM-Suite2p) over a window
   sized by tau and fs
  - Formula: (Fc - F0) / F0
  - Output is a 1D trace per neuron, not a volume
