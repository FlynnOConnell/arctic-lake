## Foreground / Background

"thresholds" defines the "level threshold" below, defaulting to `0.4`

```
Count
  │
  │  ████                          
  │  ████                          
  │  █████                    ██   
  │  ██████                  ████  
  │  ███████               ███████ 
  │  █████████           ██████████
  └──────────────────────────────────► Intensity
     ↑              ↑            ↑
    min          level         mean
   (p1)         threshold    (foreground)
   
   |←── background ──→|←── foreground ──→|
```


- min (p1):	~101	Floor of background noise
- mean: 	~113	Average of tissue signal
- mean - min:	~12	Dynamic range of your data
- level: 	~106	40% of the way from noise floor to tissue average

**clusterPT steps**
1. Load raw stack
2. Rotate (90° clockwise)
3. Dead-pixel correction - fixes hot/stuck pixels using median filter
4. Segmentation - Gaussian blur + adaptive threshold → mask
5. Apply mask - multiply corrected volume by mask
