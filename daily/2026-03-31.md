## Grid Search

### multiFuse 


┌─────────────┬───────────────┬─────────────────────────┬────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Parameter  │    Current    │          Range          │                      Why                       │
├─────────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ maskFactor  │ 1.0           │ 0.3, 0.5, 0.7, 1.0, 1.5 │ threshold aggressiveness - most visible effect │
├─────────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ blending(2) │ 4             │ 2, 4, 8, 12             │ camera transition zone width in z-planes       │
├─────────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ fusionType  │ 1 (geometric) │ 0, 1, 3                 │ adaptive vs geometric vs averaging             │
├─────────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┼────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ kernelSize  │ 5             │ 3, 5, 9                 │ mask smoothing - affects edge quality          │
└─────────────┴───────────────┴─────────────────────────┴────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Not worth searching:

- flipHFlag=1, frontFlag=2, maskFusion=1 — these are structural, not tunable
- search offsets — affects registration accuracy, not fusion quality
- percentile=5, maskMinimum=[1 100] — minor effect

Full grid: 5 × 4 × 3 × 3 = 180 combinations.

At ~6 min each that's 18 hours.

Better to do a reduced search: fix kernelSize=5, search

maskFactor × blending × fusionType = 5 × 4 × 3 = 60, about 6 hours on 8 workers.

Or even smaller — just test the most likely candidates.
